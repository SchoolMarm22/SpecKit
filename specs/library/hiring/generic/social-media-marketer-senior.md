# What We're Looking For

I need someone who understands that social media marketing at a tech company isn't about going viral — it's about building sustainable growth engines. You should have experience moving beyond vanity metrics to actual business impact: pipeline contribution, community building, and brand positioning that matters to our ICP.

Looking for someone who's been through at least one major platform algorithm change and adapted their strategy accordingly. TikTok's rise, iOS 14.5 privacy changes, Twitter's transformation — these aren't abstract industry trends, they should be chapter markers in your career story.

## Must-Haves

- Managed social budgets >$500K annually with clear ROI attribution
- Built and scaled organic social programs that generated measurable pipeline (not just engagement)
- Led social strategy through a major platform crisis or algorithm shift
- Experience with B2B social selling and community building, not just B2C awareness plays
- Can show concrete examples of content that drove actual business outcomes

## Strong Signals

- Has strong opinions about when paid social works vs. when it's a waste of money
- Experience mentoring junior marketers or managing cross-functional campaigns
- Built relationships with industry influencers that resulted in meaningful partnerships
- Understands how social fits into the broader demand gen funnel, not just top-of-funnel awareness

## Anti-Patterns

- Focuses primarily on follower counts and engagement rates instead of business metrics
- "Social media expertise" that's really just content creation without strategic thinking
- Can't articulate how their social work contributed to revenue or pipeline
- Treats every platform the same way instead of understanding unique platform dynamics
- Never had to justify social spend or prove ROI to leadership

## Interview Focus

- How they've adapted strategy when a platform changed its algorithm or policies
- Specific examples of social content or campaigns that drove business results
- How they measure and report on social media impact beyond vanity metrics
- Experience managing relationships with influencers, partners, or community members
