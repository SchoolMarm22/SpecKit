# What We're Looking For

I'm looking for someone who actually understands social platforms as a native user, not someone who learned about them in a textbook. You should be the person your friends come to when they want to know how TikTok's algorithm works or why their Instagram posts aren't getting engagement.

This isn't about follower counts or going viral — it's about understanding how to build genuine community around a brand. I need someone who can spot the difference between authentic engagement and vanity metrics, and who gets excited about testing content hypotheses rather than just posting and hoping.

## Must-Haves

- Native fluency with at least 3 major platforms (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter/X)
- Can write in different brand voices — casual Twitter vs. professional LinkedIn vs. visual Instagram
- Basic understanding of social analytics beyond likes and follows (engagement rate, reach, click-through)
- Portfolio of content you've created (personal projects, internships, volunteer work all count)

## Strong Signals

- Has grown a personal account or community project organically
- Can explain why certain content formats work better on different platforms
- Shows curiosity about platform algorithm changes and adapts accordingly
- Experience with basic design tools (Canva, Figma, or similar)

## Anti-Patterns

- Thinks "post 3 times a day" is a strategy
- Can't explain the difference between organic and paid social performance
- Only experience is managing their own personal accounts with no strategic thinking
- Believes that social media success is purely about creativity without data
- Dismisses "boring" B2B content as impossible to make engaging

## Interview Focus

- Walk me through a social campaign they admire and why it worked
- How they'd approach growing our social presence from zero (strategy, not tactics)
- Their process for staying current with platform changes and trends
- Examples of how they've used data to improve content performance
