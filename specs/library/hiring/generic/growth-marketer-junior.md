# What We're Looking For

I'm looking for someone who gets energized by numbers moving in the right direction and isn't afraid to dig into why they moved. Growth marketing at the junior level is less about having all the answers and more about asking the right questions and being obsessed with learning what works.

You don't need to come in knowing every growth hack or having run million-dollar ad campaigns. But you do need to understand that marketing without measurement is just expensive storytelling. I want someone who naturally thinks "how would we test that?" when presented with a marketing hypothesis.

## Must-Haves

- Built and analyzed at least one A/B test (could be email subject lines, landing pages, anything with a control group)
- Can pull data from analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude) without asking someone else to do it
- Understands basic funnel metrics and can explain conversion rate vs. retention rate
- Has created content that real people engaged with (blog posts, social media, email campaigns)

## Strong Signals

- Side projects or personal experiments with growth tactics
- Can explain why they chose specific metrics to track in previous work
- Asks clarifying questions about our conversion funnel during the interview
- Has opinions about attribution models (even if they're wrong, shows they're thinking about it)

## Anti-Patterns

- Talks about "viral coefficients" but can't explain how to measure them
- Only experience is with brand marketing or "awareness campaigns" 
- Thinks growth marketing is just performance ads with better targeting
- Can't articulate the difference between a leading and lagging indicator
- Defensive about testing ideas that might not work

## Interview Focus

- Walk through a campaign they ran: hypothesis, execution, results, learnings
- How they would approach improving our signup conversion rate (without knowing our current rate)
- What questions they'd want answered before launching any growth initiative
- How they stay current with growth marketing trends and tactics
