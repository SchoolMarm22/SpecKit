# What We're Looking For

I need someone who understands that SEO is a technical discipline, not just content optimization. Most "SEO specialists" I've interviewed can talk about keywords but fall apart when I ask about Core Web Vitals implementation or how they'd handle JavaScript rendering for crawlers.

The ideal candidate has lived through at least one major algorithm update (Panda, Penguin, Core Web Vitals) and can tell stories about how they adapted their strategy based on data, not panic. They should be comfortable working directly with engineers and product managers, not just handing off "SEO requirements" and hoping for the best.

## Must-Haves

- Led SEO for a site with 1M+ monthly organic sessions
- Hands-on experience with technical SEO audits using tools like Screaming Frog, Ahrefs, or Semrush at enterprise scale
- Can read and debug structured data markup (JSON-LD, Schema.org)
- Built or significantly improved page speed and Core Web Vitals metrics
- Experience with JavaScript frameworks' SEO implications (React, Vue, Angular)

## Strong Signals

- Has worked directly with engineering teams on crawl budget optimization
- Can explain the technical differences between client-side and server-side rendering for SEO
- Experience with international SEO (hreflang, ccTLDs, subdirectories)
- Has mentored junior SEO analysts or worked with content teams on SEO strategy

## Anti-Patterns

- "Technical SEO" experience that was just meta tag optimization
- Can't explain how Google's crawling and indexing actually works beyond surface-level knowledge
- Focuses only on ranking positions without connecting to business metrics
- Treats SEO as a checklist rather than an ongoing technical discipline
- Never worked with developers or doesn't understand deployment processes

## Interview Focus

- Walk through a complex technical SEO problem they solved and the engineering collaboration required
- How they prioritize SEO initiatives when development resources are limited
- Their approach to measuring and reporting SEO impact beyond rankings
- Experience with SEO during major site migrations or redesigns
